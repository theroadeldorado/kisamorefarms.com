Good 'Ol Fashioned Home Grown & Free Range

Thanksgiving turkeys raised from start to finish by the Mason Kisamore Family of Suffield, Ohio. With 15+years of experience, we raise our turkeys to make the perfect centerpiece to your holiday table.

Our turkeys enjoy a hormone and antibiotic free corn diet and spacious pasture to forage for grasses and insects as well as the ability to express natural behaviors within a flock.

The turkeys are butchered FRESH the week before Thanksgiving, therefore, specific weights cannot be pre-ordered. Customers will have choice of weights at pick up, based on what is available upon arrival. (Grocery store style; first come first served) Only customers with reservations will be guaranteed a turkey. Walk up customers may not be accommodated due to limited availability.
